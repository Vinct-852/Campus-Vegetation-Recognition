Campus vegetation classifier

Traditional ML (HOG + HSV + LBP → PCA → SVM/RF/GBDT). Runs only on your machine.

How to use

  • Choose a plant photo (JPEG, PNG, WebP, or GIF).
  • Click Predict. The image is resized to 128×128 (same as training).
  • Results show the predicted class and estimated confidence.
